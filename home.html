Two New Ways to Celebrate Birthdays

Birthday Box Movie

Send pictures, or videos for your friends. Birthday Box puts together a Movie to your friend from the messages we have received from you!

Birthday Box Gifts

Birthday Box enables you to receive and give bigger and better birthday gifts through your different friend circles. You vote on gifts, and contribute. Finally, we purchase the most voted gifts from the list, and deliver them to your friend.

How Does It Work?

You Create & We Deliver

  • Personalize your message with your video or picture
  • Be the first to know, when your closest friends have a birthday
  • Special gifts we all deserve
  • What you share with us will be kept privately and will not be published publicly, unless the Birthday Boy wants to share it publicly. Payments will be secured by Paypal.
